New York City · Juneteenth Weekend

All of it is the culture.
All of it is welcome.
All of it is here.

You already know what a fish fry means. Somewhere in your life — a backyard, a church parking lot, a block in a city you love — somebody fired up the grease, the music got loud, and everybody who was anybody showed up. That's not just a memory. That's a tradition. And every Juneteenth weekend, we bring it to Randall's Island for the entire culture. What started as a backyard gathering at the KO Chapter, ΩΨΦ house in Harlem has grown into one of New York's most anticipated summer celebrations — drawing over 2,000 people to Randall's Island and counting.

What to Expect

The Full Experience

The Turn Up

The DJs are going to take you through it all. DJ Self of Power 105.1 and DJ Freedom aka Big New York don't miss. The food vendors are going to have you going back for thirds. The waterfront air is going to hit different when you're surrounded by 2,000+ people who all came to the same place for the same reason.

🎵
Live DJs All Day

DJ Self of Power 105.1 and DJ Freedom aka Big New York hold it down from noon to close. No lull. No filler. Just the soundtrack your summer deserves.

🍤
Legendary Fish

Some of NYC's best food vendors pull up with the real thing. The fish fry tradition elevated — fresh, hot, and worth every bite.

☀️
Premium Day Party Energy

Open air, summer sun, Randall's Island waterfront. The vibe hits from the moment you walk through the gates. Bring your lawn chair, bring your crew.

🛍️
Vendor Village

Curated marketplace featuring Black-owned businesses, HBCU merch, art, and more. Shop, discover, and support while you celebrate.

Welcome to the Yard

At some point during the day, you're going to see a group of men or women line up on the grass and do something you've probably only seen on a screen — if you've seen it at all. The stepping. The strolling. The call and response that sounds like it's coming from somewhere deep. That's the legacy of the historically Black fraternities and sororities — the Divine Nine — showing up the way they've always shown up. Yard energy. Homecoming energy. If you went to an HBCU you already know. If you didn't, welcome to the yard.

All of Us

You're going to see sisters in every version of themselves — locs out, natural coils, bone straight, waist-length, wigs, weaves, all of it — and every single one of them showing up and showing out. You're going to see brothers dressed for the summer. Families, friend groups, couples, and solo flyers who knew exactly what they were walking into.

🤝
The Culture in Full Effect

This is Juneteenth weekend done right. Celebration, connection, community — the whole culture shows up and shows out.

🥂
VIP Tent Experience

Reserved tent, premium platters, spirits, and the best view on the island. Ten-deep and ready to celebrate? This is your lane.

Beyond the Party

Community
Service

The Fish Fry Festival is a celebration — and a contribution. A portion of every ticket sold goes directly toward supporting the communities that have always shown up for us.

In partnership with Engage Reform, KIPP NYC Charter Schools, and the Kappa Omicron Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc., proceeds from this event fund The Ladders Program — a 6-week Algebra Regents sprint designed to help NYC high school students cross the threshold to graduation.

Every student climbing deserves someone holding the ladder.
